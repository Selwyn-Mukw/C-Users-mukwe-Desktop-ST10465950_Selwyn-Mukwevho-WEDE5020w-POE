About Mbali Bakes

Mbali Bakes is a homegrown bakery founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. After losing her job, Mbali returned to her hometown of Klerksdorp, where she rediscovered her passion for baking. She began selling freshly baked scones and muffins in the streets of Kanana, and her goods quickly became community favourites — often selling out within hours. With strong local support and growing demand, Mbali turned her passion into a sustainable business, creating Mbali Bakes.

Our vision is to become a beloved household name in South Africa’s baking industry — starting from Kanana and expanding into wider markets — while inspiring others to turn passion into purpose.

Our mission is to bring warmth, comfort, and joy to the community through freshly baked, homemade goods crafted with love and care. We aim to empower local entrepreneurship while delivering consistent quality with every bite.

Mbali Bakes serves:

  • Local families and individuals in Kanana
  • Early-morning commuters and workers
  • Event planners and caterers for parties, meetings, churches, and functions
