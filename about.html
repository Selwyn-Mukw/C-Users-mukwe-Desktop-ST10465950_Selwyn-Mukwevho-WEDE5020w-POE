Who is Mbali bakes ?

 
    Mbali Bakes is a homegrown bakery business founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. After losing her job,
    Mbali returned to her hometown of Klerksdorp, where she rediscovered her passion for baking.
    She began selling freshly baked scones and muffins each morning on the streets of Kanana. 
    Her baked goods quickly gained popularity in the community, often selling out within hours.
    Recognizing the strong demand and support from locals,
    Mbali saw an opportunity to turn her passion into a sustainable business — and thus, Mbali Bakes was born

Our Vision 

    Our vision is to become a beloved household name in South Africa’s baking industry starting from the heart
    of Kanana and expanding into wider markets — while inspiring others to turn passion into purpose and business.

Our Mission 

    At Mbali Bakes, our mission is to bring warmth, comfort, and joy to our community through freshly baked, 
    homemade goods made with love, passion, and care. We aim to empower local entrepreneurship while delivering
    quality and consistency with every bite.

Target Audience 

    Mbali bakes cater for:
	* Local community members: Individuals and families in Kanana
	* Commuters and Workers: Early morning customers such as school children, and workers on their way to work who are looking for a small treaty.
	* Event planners and catering: local event organisers, parties, functions, meetings, churches or any community groups in need of affordable and tasty baked foods
    Mbali bakes focus on both everyday customers and occasional bulk orders.
